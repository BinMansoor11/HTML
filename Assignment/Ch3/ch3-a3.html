Muffin Recipe

A basic recipe. You can add pretty much everything to it.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 200 caster sugar
  • 250ml milk
  • 125ml vegetable oil
  • 400g plain floor
  • 3tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to Gas Mark 6 or a medium temperature. Line Muffin tray with paper muffin cases.
  2. Beat the eggs in a mixing bowl and then mix in the oil and milk. Add the sugar and whisk untill dissolved.
  3. Sift in the dry ingredients: flour, baking powder salt and mix untill smooth. NB: add any extra ingredients now eg, chocolate chips/fruits
  4. Fill muffin cases TWO THIRDS full(this is important for the perfect size muffin) and bake in the middle of the oven for 25 minutes (approx. depending on your oven)
  5. Leave to cool ( or enjoy warm! )
